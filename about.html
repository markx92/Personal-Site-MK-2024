Hello and welcome!

I'm Mark Kardjian, born in Los Angeles and raised in the vibrant San Fernando Valley. With a rich Armenian heritage, I've always been deeply connected to my roots and community.

I pursued an undergraduate degree in Civil Engineering at the University of California, Irvine, where I developed a strong foundation in structural principles and a passion for building and design. After graduation, I embarked on an eight-year journey as a licensed structural engineer with KCE Matrix. My experience there honed my skills in problem-solving and project management, working on diverse and challenging projects.

Seeking to blend my engineering expertise with my growing interest in business, I am currently pursuing an MBA at Georgetown University, specializing in Real Estate Finance. This new path reflects my commitment to understanding the financial and developmental aspects of real estate, aiming to make impactful decisions in the industry.

This site is intended to share info about who I am to those that are interested, please browse at your liesure. Thank you for visiting my page and taking the time to learn more about my journey and aspirations. I look forward to connecting and exploring potential opportunities together.
